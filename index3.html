BORACAY INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY ASSOCIATION

Group of young professionals in IT field that is based in Boracay Island.

The purpose of Boracay Information Technology Association (BITA) is to build relationship between the community and other sectors of society (business, governments, hotels, resorts, etc.) within Boracay and nearby provinces. Our association is composed of IT Specialists in the island of Boracay that aims to deepen the general public awareness and understanding of technology through attending technological updates, facilitate technological advancement and partnership with various technological vendors. Our program also involves connecting our members through technology, networking opportunities and in developing and supporting local community and business sectors. Through the use of technology and social media, we aim to advocate patronage, community service, volunteerism and to ensure that useful information can benefit the communities, businesses and general public.
